Who We Are

C Maritime Solutions, a subsidiary of the Catro Group of Companies, provides comprehensive crewing services to the international shipping industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional crewing services to the international maritime industry by recruiting, training, and deploying highly skilled professionals. We are committed to fostering long-term partnerships, ensuring the highest standards of safety and efficiency, and driving innovation to meet the evolving needs of the global shipping community.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of innovative and reliable crewing solutions, empowering the global maritime industry with skilled professionals and contributing to a safer and more sustainable future at sea.

Empowering Our Customers, Inspiring Our Community

Your trusted partner in maritime logistics and shipping services.
